Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1. Introduction

Welcome to QR Zap! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines the permissions we request, how we handle the data, and how ads are served in the app.

2. Permissions We Request

Our application requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera – To scan QR codes.
  • Write External Storage – To save QR code images in your gallery.
  • Read External Storage – To read stored QR code images.
  • Read Media Images – To access images for scanning QR codes from your device.
  • Read Contacts – This permission is required if a QR code contains contact information (like a phone number, email, or name). The app uses this permission to read the contact details so that you can choose to save them directly to your device's contacts list.
  • Write Contacts – This permission allows the app to add or modify contact details on your device. If a QR code contains contact information, the app will request this permission to add or update the contact in your device's contacts database.

3. What Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information apart from the permissions mentioned above. These permissions are solely used for scanning and saving QR codes. No personal data is stored or shared with third parties.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data solely for the purpose of creating and scanning QR codes within the app. Any data related to QR codes, such as images, is stored locally on your device and not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

5. Ads in the App

Our app uses third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display ads. These services may collect certain information to provide personalized ads. Below is how ads work in the app:

  • Ad Providers – We use third-party ad networks like Google AdMob to serve ads in the app. These networks may collect information such as your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and usage data to deliver relevant ads.
  • Ad Tracking – Ad providers may use cookies, device identifiers, or other tracking technologies to collect data for ad-targeting purposes.
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads – You can opt-out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's advertising settings. For example, on Android, you can go to Settings > Google > Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

6. Data Retention

Your QR code images and other data are stored locally and are retained until you choose to delete them from your device. Ad providers may retain data as per their own privacy policies.

You have the right to deny any permissions requested by the app. If you choose to deny certain permissions, please note that the app’s functionality may be limited. Additionally, you can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings.
