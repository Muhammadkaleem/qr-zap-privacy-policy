Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

CalmPath ("we", "us") is a mental-wellness app that offers an AI companion for supportive conversation, mood tracking, and private journaling. This policy explains what we collect, how we use and protect it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using CalmPath you agree to this policy.

CalmPath is not a medical service. It does not provide diagnosis, therapy, or treatment, is not a substitute for professional care, and is not for emergencies. We are not a HIPAA-covered entity. If you are in crisis, contact your local emergency number or a crisis line — the app also lists crisis resources.

How we use it

  • To provide the app: generate AI responses, store your journal/mood/chat history, and show your insights.
  • To keep you safe: run a crisis-safety filter and surface resources.
  • To operate and improve: understand which features help (via privacy-preserving analytics) and fix crashes.
  • To communicate: account emails such as confirmation, password reset, and data-export links.

We do not sell your personal information, and your content is never used to train AI models.

Encryption & security

Your conversations, journal entries, and mood notes are encrypted at rest. Access is protected by row-level security so your data is readable only by you (and the service components needed to operate the app). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we design for data-minimization and least privilege.

Your rights & choices

  • Access & export: request a full export of your data in the app (Settings → Privacy & data).
  • Delete: permanently delete your account and data in the app (Settings → Delete account) or via our web deletion page. Deletion removes your account, conversations, journals, mood history, and insights. Anonymized crisis-safety records (with no link to you) may be retained for auditing.
  • Correct: update your display name in the app.
  • Notifications: turn reminders on or off any time.

Data retention

We keep your data until you delete it or your account. When you delete your account, your personal data is removed promptly; anonymized safety records may be retained as described above.

Children

CalmPath is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

International users

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own by us and our providers. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
