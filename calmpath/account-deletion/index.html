Delete your account & data

This page explains how to permanently delete your CalmPath account and the data associated with it, what gets removed, and what we retain.

Option 1 — Delete it in the app (fastest)

  1. Open CalmPath and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete account.
  3. Confirm you understand it can't be undone, then re-enter your password.

Your account and data are deleted immediately upon confirmation.

Option 2 — Request it by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address on your account and we'll process the deletion:

Email support@calmpath.app

We verify ownership of the account email, then delete the account. Requests are completed within 30 days (usually within a few days).

What is deleted

  • Your account and profile (email, display name)
  • All chat conversations with Calm
  • Your mood check-ins and history
  • Your journal entries
  • Your wellness insights
  • Your notification preferences and subscription record

What is retained

  • Anonymized crisis-safety records — for everyone's safety we keep a record that a safety event occurred (severity and time only, with no link back to you and no message content).
  • Anonymized, aggregated usage analytics — de-identified event counts, never any message, journal, or mood-note content.

We may also retain limited records where required by law. We never keep the content of your conversations, journal, or mood notes after deletion.

Active subscription? Deleting your CalmPath account does not cancel a Google Play (or App Store) subscription. Cancel it first in your store account: Play Store subscriptions.
